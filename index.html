Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Flingo ("we", "us", "our"), your privacy is our foundation — not an afterthought. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Flingo, we may collect:

  • Account information: Name, date of birth, email address, phone number, gender, and profile photos.
  • Profile data: Interests, bio, preferences, and other optional information you choose to share.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app, matches, likes, and messages (metadata only).
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Location data: General city/region only — we never track precise real-time location without consent.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To match you with compatible people on the platform.
  • To verify your identity and prevent fake profiles.
  • To improve our matching algorithms and app experience.
  • To send you service-related notifications (not marketing spam).
  • To comply with legal obligations under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

3. We Never Sell Your Data

Flingo does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers. We show no ads — and your data is never used for advertising purposes by us or any partner.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain data only as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, your personal data is removed within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your data at any time.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy questions or requests, please email support@theflingo.in.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Flingo uses minimal, essential cookies to make the app work. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help apps remember preferences and session state.

Cookies we use

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your session.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings like notification preferences.
  • Security cookies: Help us detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

What we never use

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics that identify you as an individual.
